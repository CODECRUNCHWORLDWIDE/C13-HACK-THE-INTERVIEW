# Week 5 — Challenges

1. **[Handle the trick question](./challenge-01-handle-the-trick-question.md)** — drill the four classic HM trick/stress questions in a focused 30 minutes (~60 min total with prep)

Optional but high-leverage. The four stress questions — conflict, weakness, why-you-over-others, first-90-days — show up in roughly 80% of HM screens, and they are the most-skipped prep area. The challenge pulls them out of the rest of the week's voice reps and gives each a focused drill.

Pairs naturally with Exercise 3 (the pair mock): the four stress questions are typically asked in the mock, and you'll perform measurably better on them after this challenge than before.
