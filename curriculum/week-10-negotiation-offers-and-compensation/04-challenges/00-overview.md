# Week 10 — Challenges

1. **[The Exploding Offer Drill](./challenge-01-the-exploding-offer.md)** — recognise the artificial 48-hour deadline, ask for an extension calmly, and walk away cleanly if it is refused (~45-60 min, Hard)
2. **[The Decline-Gracefully Email](./challenge-02-the-decline-email.md)** — write the email that closes the offer without closing the door on the recruiter you will see again in five years (~30-45 min, Medium)

Optional but high-value. Both rehearse the moves that happen *around* the negotiation — the deadline pressure and the graceful exit — and both produce a written artefact worth keeping for the day the real offer lands.
