# Challenge 1 — Reverse-engineer a job posting

**Time:** ~75 minutes. **Difficulty:** Medium.

## Problem

Take **one real job posting** from a Tier-A company on your list. Reverse-engineer:

1. The 5-8 hard requirements (must-haves).
2. The 5-8 nice-to-haves.
3. The 3-5 specific deal-breakers (often subtle).
4. The likely *unstated* expectations.
5. The leveling implied by the role (junior / mid / senior / staff).
6. The most likely *first* interview round, given the company's reputation.
7. Three things you'd say in a recruiter screen to position yourself well.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] You pick one specific posting (link in your write-up).
- [ ] You produce `challenge-01-decoded.md` with each numbered section above.
- [ ] For each "hard requirement," you include either: "I have this" with evidence, or "I don't have this but will address by…"
- [ ] You identify at least 2 *unstated* expectations from reading between the lines.

## How to read between the lines

A job posting is written by a recruiter, edited by HR, with input from the hiring manager. Each layer adds noise. Things to notice:

- **Order matters.** Skills listed first are weighted higher.
- **Verb choice.** "Comfortable with" is weaker than "proficient in" is weaker than "expert in."
- **Years phrasing.** "5+ years" usually means "we want senior"; "3-5 years" means "we want mid."
- **Tech listed but not detailed.** Often a buzzword-list. Pay more attention to the project descriptions.
- **"Bonus points for…"** is rarely a deal-breaker but often the differentiator.
- **Unstated:** if the company is known to use specific tech (e.g., everyone knows Stripe uses Ruby), and the posting doesn't mention it — that's an inferred requirement.

## Stretch

- Find a *worse* version of the same role at a different company. Compare the wording.
- Find someone who works in that role at that company on LinkedIn. Look at their actual background. Does it match the posting?

## Submission

Commit `challenge-01-decoded.md` to your portfolio under `c13-week-01/challenge-01/`.

## Why this matters

If you don't decode the posting, you over-apply to roles you can't get and under-apply to roles you'd thrive in. After doing this challenge 5-10 times across the cycle, you'll do it in your head in 90 seconds.
