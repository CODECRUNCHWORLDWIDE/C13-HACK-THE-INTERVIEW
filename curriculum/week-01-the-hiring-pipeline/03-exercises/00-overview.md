# Week 1 — Exercises

Three structured tasks. Each produces a tangible artifact you'll use for the rest of the cycle.

1. **[Exercise 1 — Build the spreadsheet](./exercise-01-build-the-spreadsheet.md)** — set up your tracking dashboard (~45 min)
2. **[Exercise 2 — Draft 30 companies](./exercise-02-draft-30-companies.md)** — research and populate your tiered target list (~90 min)
3. **[Exercise 3 — Write five outreach messages](./exercise-03-write-five-outreach-messages.md)** — actually write them, don't send yet (~60 min)

## How to work them

Do them in order. Each builds on the last.

Don't skip the spreadsheet — every other exercise feeds into it.

**Don't send any outreach until you've completed all three exercises and reviewed your messages with at least one other person.** Sending bad outreach burns relationships you'll want later.
