# Week 7 — Challenges

1. **[Shadow a Real Loop](./challenge-01-shadow-a-real-loop.md)** — script, send, and track an informational request to a working engineer who runs interviews, to see the loop from the interviewer side (~60 min plus the conversation if accepted)
2. **[After-Action Template](./challenge-02-after-action-template.md)** — build a reusable, fast-to-fill after-action you complete within 2 hours of every loop for the rest of the cycle (~75 min)

Optional but high-leverage. Challenge 1 buys you the one view of the loop you can't get from Glassdoor or your own seat — what the interviewer actually does and what gets written in the debrief; expect to send 3-5 requests for 1 accepted conversation, and treat the polite declines as part of the practice. Challenge 2 turns Exercise 3's self-eval into a leaner, evergreen template — the artefact that lets each loop teach the next one. Both produce documents you'll keep reaching for through Weeks 8-9 and into your next cycle.
