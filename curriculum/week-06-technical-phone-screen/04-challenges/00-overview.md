# Week 6 — Challenges

1. **[Do an easy LeetCode on CoderPad, narrated](./challenge-01-do-an-easy-leetcode-on-coderpad-narrated.md)** — a single narrated easy LeetCode in a focused 30 minutes, recorded (~30-45 min total)

Optional but high-leverage. The challenge isolates the single load-bearing skill of the week — narrating one easy problem cleanly on a real shared editor — and forces a focused 30-minute rep without the broader scaffolding of the exercises.

Pairs naturally with Exercise 2 (the narration drill): the challenge is the same shape as one slot of Exercise 2 but with stricter constraints (no examples-list to pick from, harder timebox, mandatory recording review). Best done **between** Exercise 2 and Exercise 1 — Exercise 2 builds the muscle, the challenge tests it under pressure, Exercise 1 puts it under a live partner.

It is also a useful drop-in any week of the rest of the cycle. Once you have a real phone screen scheduled, run the challenge the morning of the call as a 30-minute warm-up; the muscle is fresh.
